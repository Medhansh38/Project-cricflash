| ABOUT US

Reliving Cricket's Greatest Moments

At CricFlash, we live and breathe cricket. Born from a passion for the game's most electrifying moments, we've built a digital sanctuary for fans who believe cricket isn't just a sport—it's an emotion.

Our Story

Founded in 2025 by a team of cricket archivists and tech enthusiasts, CricFlash began as a passion project to preserve the magic of iconic matches. Today, we've grown into the web's most comprehensive cricket moments library, serving over 250,000 fans monthly.

What We Offer

  • Frame-by-frame archives of legendary matches
  • Curated collections (World Cup, IPL, Test classics)
  • Mobile-optimized video experience

Our Mission

To ensure no cricket moment is ever forgotten—from Tendulkar's straight drives to Stokes' Headingley heroics. We're building cricket's living memory, one epic moment at a time.

"Because every boundary, every wicket, every victory deserves to be relived."
